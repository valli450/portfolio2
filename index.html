About Val

Val spent years around crews and small trade businesses, then started building the software they actually open on the job.

That vantage point shapes everything here: interfaces that survive gloves and glare, sign-in flows that do not assume an email address, and money screens that answer a contractor's real questions. Who owes me. What did this job cost. What do I put aside for April.

Before software there was a BA in Economics with a minor in IT from MGIMO, the Moscow State Institute of International Relations, and before that, competitive wrestling at a school that fed candidates to the national team. The engineering foundation came the long way too: Codecademy's Front-End Engineer career path, plus JavaScript and Python certificates, all earned before the AI wave, not after it.

JobFlow and JobFlow Consulting are the first two public case studies. The rest of the shelf is being written up now.
